A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab ans see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick abd easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmaks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you to find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmaks.

Share your bookmaks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
